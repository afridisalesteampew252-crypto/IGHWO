Message from Founder

Qaisar Zaman

Our mission is simple: To provide justice and relief to those who cannot afford it. Based at the heart of Peshawar in Lady Reading Hospital, we witness the daily struggles of patients and their families. Insaf Global Hand is here to ensure no one fights their medical battles alone.

Our Specialized Wings

LRH Patient Support

Providing essential medicines and blood donation services for patients at Lady Reading Hospital.

Dastarkhwan

Free meal distribution for the attendants and families of underprivileged patients.

Emergency Relief

Immediate financial and logistical aid for critical surgeries and emergency cases.
